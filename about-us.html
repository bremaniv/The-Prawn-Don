The Prawn Don: About Us

Your Go-To Resource for All Things Aquatics

Founded in 2025 by Don Brenden, The Prawn Don's mission is simple: to make the aquarium hobby more accessible and enjoyable. We're dedicated to providing the best information, advice, and product recommendations for everyone, from your very first tank to your most ambitious project. We know how frustrating it can be to sift through countless websites to find reliable information. That's why we’ve built a single, trusted hub for all your needs. Our journey is a shared one. We're not just here to teach—we're here to learn alongside you. The Prawn Don is always on the quest to discover more, so you can focus on building a happy and healthy aquarium. Remember, Don't Just Get It Done. Get It Don!

The Inspiration

Brenden, known as The Prawn Don, inherited his love for the aquatic world from his father, The Don Father. Growing up, water was never just water—it was alive with tadpoles, crayfish, bettas, and eventually, shrimp. What began as a childhood fascination became a lifelong calling. His breeding journey started with betta fish, where the mystery of genetics and the thrill of seeing new phenotypes come to life captured his imagination. Every spawn was an experiment in possibility, proof that nature held more stories than could ever be told. But it was in dwarf shrimp—especially Caridina cantonensis—that Brenden found his true canvas. Their vibrant, psychedelic patterns and rapid life cycles offered endless opportunities to explore, create, and innovate. For Brenden, breeding isn’t just about shrimp. It’s about curiosity, creativity, and leaving a mark on a hobby that’s still writing its future.
